    About

    I moved to San Francisco in 2007 from Vero Beach, Florida. I became a baker in 2002 and eventually became a Pastry Chef. The early hours, physical labor, and culture of the restaurant industry became draining. That led to the search for something else. I was craving a career that was challenging, creative, and something I could really sink into.

    Early 2015 I started doing online tutorials in programming. A spark ignited. Programming fit all my criteria. I quit my job and started learning online full-time. Now, it's time for the next step, a sling-shot into my new career. I couldn't be more excited for this next chapter!
